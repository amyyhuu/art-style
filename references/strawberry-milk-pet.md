# Strawberry Milk Pet Style

Use this reference when the user asks for Strawberry Milk Pet, mimi pet, soft bunny cafe, kawaii dessert pet, pastel study buddy, or cozy pet companion art.

## Visual DNA

Strawberry Milk Pet is a soft companion-pet art style for cute mascots, dessert characters, stickers, icons, and cozy app assets. It should feel like a tiny helper pet from a strawberry milk cafe: gentle, plush, sweet, clean, and emotionally warm.

Core traits:

- rounded pet silhouette, often bunny-like, with small paws and soft ears
- white cream body, strawberry-pink blush, warm cocoa linework
- milk-gloss highlights and soft paper-like shadows
- dessert and cafe props: strawberry slices, bows, syrup ribbons, whipped cream, mochi pearls, tiny spoons, bingsu bowls, cookies, milk cartons
- cozy expression: curious, encouraging, slightly shy, never sharp or chaotic
- polished asset finish: readable at small sizes, clean edges, clear subject

## Palette

- ink: `#2b2020`
- soft cocoa outline: `#4f3934`
- muted cocoa: `#7a5f5d`
- milk white: `#fffdf8`
- cream surface: `#fffafc`
- strawberry milk background: `#ffd8d2`
- strawberry pink: `#ff9aa2`
- strong berry pink: `#ef6f79`
- blush pink: `#ffafc5`
- bow pink: `#ff88b2`
- pale syrup: `#ffc7c2`
- milky peach: `#ffe6e2`
- dessert outline: `#f4c3bf`

Use color contrast through cream, cocoa, and white rather than heavy dark shadows.

## Shape And Finish

Use:

- large simple head/body masses
- oval eyes or tiny dot eyes
- short curved mouth
- cheek ovals with translucent blush
- pillowy paws, floppy ears, soft bow knots
- rounded dessert components layered like soft stickers
- subtle pencil or watercolor irregularity in outlines
- gentle drop shadow or paper shadow
- small glossy highlights on milk, syrup, eyes, and fruit

Avoid:

- hard angular geometry
- heavy black outlines
- photorealistic animal anatomy
- gritty texture, horror, cyberpunk, neon, metallic armor
- crowded backgrounds that hide the pet silhouette
- visible text unless requested

## Composition Recipes

Sticker: isolated pet on a transparent background, centered full body or three-quarter view, one clear prop, clean outline, soft shadow.

Icon: close-up face or bust, one readable symbol such as berry, bow, or milk drop, strong silhouette, minimal detail.

Scene: pet large in foreground, simple strawberry milk cafe, study desk, dessert tray, or soft pastel room background.

Pattern: repeat small pets, berries, bows, milk drops, spoons, stars, and dessert pieces with airy spacing.

Character sheet: front, side, back, expression row, accessory row, consistent proportions across poses.

## Prompt Formula

Use this structure for image-generation prompts:

```text
[format] of [subject], original Strawberry Milk Pet style, [pose or action], soft white and strawberry-pink palette, warm cocoa sketch outlines, rounded plush proportions, tiny paws, glossy milk highlights, translucent blush cheeks, dessert/cafe accents, cozy gentle expression, clean polished asset, [background], cozy pastel lighting. Avoid photorealism, heavy black outlines, harsh neon, horror, clutter, accidental text, watermark, logo, and signature.
```

## Quality Checklist

- The pet is immediately readable as cute and soft.
- Strawberry milk colors are present but not one-note.
- The face is warm, gentle, and simple.
- The silhouette works at thumbnail size.
- The output does not accidentally include unreadable text.
- Transparent assets have clean edges.
